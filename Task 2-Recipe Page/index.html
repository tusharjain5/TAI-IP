Chhole Bhature

A popular Punjabi dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). Cook chickpeas with onions, tomatoes, and spices, and pair with fluffy bhature for a satisfying meal.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa: A South Indian favorite. Spread fermented rice-lentil batter thinly on a hot griddle. Add spicy potato filling, fold, and serve with coconut chutney and sambar.

Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani: A rich and creamy North Indian lentil curry. Slow-cook black lentils and kidney beans with aromatic spices, onions, tomatoes, and cream. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Momos

A Momos: Delectable Tibetan dumplings stuffed with spiced meat or vegetables, enveloped in dough, then steamed or fried to perfection. Enjoy with tangy dipping sauces for an unforgettable culinary experience.

Maggi

Maggi: Boil noodles in water, add seasoning, and stir in vegetables or protein of choice. Simmer until cooked, then enjoy a quick and tasty meal.
